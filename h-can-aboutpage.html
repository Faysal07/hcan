About Us

Born in 2017, our company specializes in the production and wholesale marketing of Cannabis CBD. Made up of a team of enthusiasts, our mixity is our strength. Our employees are from all walks of life to guarantee a quality service at all levels.

Our agronomy engineer supervises the production team and ensures the selection of the best varieties, ensuring optimal conditions so that they can express the best of their genetic potential.

Our logistics specialist works in collaboration with the production team to optimize the processing of flowers and the management of different processes.

Finally, our administrative team oversees operations and maintains a friendly client relationship in a professional environment to meet all the demands and needs of our partners, friends and clients.

Quality

Our mission is to share our passion for the cannabis plant by offering the highest quality natural products to the companies and communities we serve.

Natural

At H-Can, we favor the respect of the plant and the consumer, that's why we only use organic and natural solutions, to offer you the best flowers ... Cultivated with love!

Excellency

our satisfaction is our happiness, which is why we are constantly looking for the best varieties and improving our production techniques. By focusing on transparency and availability, we aim to achieve a level of excellence.
